How to Play: Game of 27

  1. Player can roll the dice as many times as they'd like, either once at a time, or in sets of multiple rolls
  2. After each roll of the dice, the table will display which side the dice landed on
  3. When done rolling the dice, the player must guess if the dice is weighted or not based on the table results
  4. After the player makes their guess, the actual weight of the dice will display
  5. To start a new game, start rolling the dice again
