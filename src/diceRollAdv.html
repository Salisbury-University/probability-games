How to Play

Game Setup Single Roll Multiple Rolls Weighted/Nonweighted Guess Weighted Side Guess Probability

Welcome to the Dice Roll Advanced!

    In this game, players can roll a six-sided die and make guesses about whether the die is weighted or not. After rolling the die 10 times, players will have the opportunity to guess if the die is weighted or not.

Single Roll

    To roll the die, players can simply click the Roll Dice button located on the left side of the page. This will roll the die a single time.

Multiple Rolls

    To roll the die multiple times, players can enter the desired number of rolls in the input box provided. After entering the number of rolls, they can click the Roll Dice button located to the right of the input box. This will roll the dice the designated number of rolls.

Guessing Weighted Or Not Weighted

    After rolling the die 10 or more times, the player will be prompted to guess whether the die is weighted or not. If the die is not weighted, the game will reset, and the player can continue rolling.

Guessing Which Side Is Weighted

    If the die is weighted, the player will be prompted to select which die face is weighted. This can be done by choosing the corresponding card or picture representing each side of the die.

Guessing The Probability Of Weighted Side Being Rolled

    In this version, players will also be asked to guess the probability of the die landing on the weighted side. Along with guessing which side of the die is weighted, players will provide their estimation of the likelihood of the die landing on that specific side.
