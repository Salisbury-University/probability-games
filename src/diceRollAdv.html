Welcome to the Dice Roll Game!

    Welcome to the dice roll simulation game! This game is designed to not only provide hours of fun and entertainment, but also to teach players an important lesson about the weight of dice and how it can affect the outcome of a roll.

    In this game, players can flip a virtual die a large number of times, and can guess whether the die is weighted or not. If the die is indeed weighted, players can also guess which of the six sides of the dice is the weighted side. Once players guess the correct side, they can also guess the probability of how likely the weighted side of the dice is landed on.

    This game is perfect for anyone who wants to understand the concept of die weight and how it can influence the outcome of a die roll. So what are you waiting for? Lets start rolling!

Single Roll

    Our game's "Single Roll" button enables players to quickly and easily flip a die and see the result. The gray backgorund behind the side of the die thats landed on will turn green to indicate which side was landed on. This feature provides an opportunity for players to test their luck and try to guess the outcome of the roll. Whether you're new to the game or an experienced player, the "Single Roll" button is a convenient and fun way to play.

Multiple Rolls

    Our game features a "Multiple Rolls" button that allows players to roll the die multiple times with a single click. This feature enables players to experiment with different numbers of rolls and observe how it affects the outcome of the game. Players can enter the amount of rolls into a text box next to the multiple rolls button they'd like to see occur, and the die will roll that amount of times. The "Multiple Rolls" button is an excellent way to test your luck and improve your understanding of how the game works. Whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned player, the "Multiple Rolls" button is a fun and convenient feature that makes our die roll simulation game enjoyable to play.

Guessing Weighted Or Not Weighted

    When the player is satisfied with the amount of times they have rolled the die, they can either click the weighted button or the not weighted button at the bottom of the screen. If the player thinks that the die is weighted, then they should click the weighted button, and if not then click the not weighted button. If the die is in fact not weighted, then the game is over and the player can start rolling the die again in a new game. If the dice is in fact weighted, the player will move on to the next step of the game.

Guessing Which Side Is Weighted

    If the dice is weighted, the player will move on to this step of the game. The player will be able to guess which of the six sides of the dice is the weighted side. To do this, the player will click the card containing the side of the dice that they think is weighted. Once the player selects the side that is weighted, they will be able to move on to the next step of the game.

Guessing The Probability Of Weighted Side Being Rolled

    Once the player selects the side that is weighted, they will be able to guess the probabiltiy of how likely it is that weighted side is landed on. In order to do this, the player will click a dropdown to see all of the possible options. The player will select the option that they think is the correct probability, and click the submit button. Once the player chooses the correct probability, the game will be over, and the player can start rolling the die again in a new game.
