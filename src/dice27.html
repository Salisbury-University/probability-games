How to Play

  1. Player rolls dice.
  2. Player asked what is the max number of piles that can be made based on dice roll.
  3. Player asked how many chips remain after creating piles.
  4. If the remainder is greater than 0 the player gets those chips as points.
  5. Swap to the other player.
  6. Repeat steps 1 - 5 until the total number of chips is 0 then the player with the most chips wins.
