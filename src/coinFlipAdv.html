Welcome to the Coin Flip Simulator!

    Through this simulator, players can simulate a large number of coin flips and games to observe the average distribution of coin flips across those games. By running multiple simulations, players can gain insights into the expected outcomes and statistical patterns of coin flips over an extended period.
