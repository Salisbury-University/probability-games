How to Play

  1. Player's each take turns adding their chips to the numbered cards, until there are no chips remaining in their piles
  2. Player 1 rolls the two dice and adds the sum of the dice.
    3. If Player 1 has a chip on the card equal to the sum of the dice remove the chip and get a point.
    Otherwise, move onto player 2's roll
  3. Player 2 rolls the two dice and adds the sum of the dice.
    4. Now, if Player 2 has a chip on the card equal to the sum of the dice remove the chip and get a point.
    Otherwise, it becomes player 1's turn to roll again
  4. Continue rolling the dice and removing chips, until one player removes all of their chips
  5. First player to remove all of their chips from their cards wins!
  6. Try to place your chips where you think the dice will roll more often than not!
