Welcome to the Coin Flip Game!

    Welcome to the coin flip simulation game! This game is designed to not only provide hours of fun and entertainment, but also to teach players an important lesson about the weight of coins and how it can affect the outcome of a flip.

    In this game, players can flip a virtual coin an large number of times and can guess whether the coin is weighted or not. If the coin is indeed weighted, players can also make a guess about the degree of the weight.

    This game is perfect for anyone who wants to understand the concept of coin weight and how it can influence the outcome of a coin flip. So what are you waiting for? Lets start flipping!

Single Flip

    Our game's "Single Flip" button enables players to quickly and easily flip a coin and see the result. This feature provides an opportunity for players to test their luck and try to guess the outcome of the flip. Whether you're new to the game or an experienced player, the "Single Flip" button is a convenient and fun way to play.

Multiple Flips

    Our game features a "Multiple Flip" button that allows players to flip the coin multiple times with a single click. This feature enables players to experiment with different numbers of flips and observe how it affects the outcome of the game. The "Multiple Flip" button is an excellent way to test your luck and improve your understanding of how the game works. Whether you're a newcomer or a seasoned player, the "Multiple Flip" button is a fun and convenient feature that makes our coin flip simulation game enjoyable to play.

Removing Chips

    When the player rolls the dice, they must add up the sum of the two dice. Once they get the sum, they check the card labeled with that sum. If they have a chip on that card, they can click their chip on that card, and it will be removed from it.

Change Coin (Optional)

    Player can change image of the coin by selecting the center dropdown menu and choosing from the list of possible images.
