How to Play

Game Information Select A Door Switch or Keep Display Stats Reset Game

Welcome to Monty Hall Problem!

    Below there are three doors, and there are two vegetables and one dessert hidden behind the doors.
    You select one door and then a vegetable is revealed. You are then given an option to either keep your current door or switch to the other unopened door. After which the door you choose is revealed.
    The purpose of this game is to help educate you on probability.

Select a Door

    To first start the game, you will have to click on any of the three doors present!

    After which, you will be prompted to click on another door, this door contains the Losing Prize, open that door to reveal the losing prize.

Switch or Keep

    After you reveal the door with the losing prize, you will then be asked to either keep the door You chose, or select the other unopened door. You can choose the door you want by clicking on it.

    After you click the door you want, you will then either lose or win. The door will open, and then the last unopened door will be opened.

Display Stats

    In order to see how you have been doing this play session, you have to click "Show Stats".

    While the stats are displayed, you cannot play the game. In order to continue playing the game, you have to click "Continue Playing".

Reset Game

    If you would like to reset your stats, click "Reset Game" right next to "Show Stats" or "Continue Playing".

    This will set you back to how the game appeared when you first played.
