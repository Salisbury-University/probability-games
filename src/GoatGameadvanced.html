How to Play

Game Information Enter Values Run Simulation Reset Game

Welcome to Monty Hall Advanced Problem!

    Below there are three doors, and there are two losing prizes and one winning prize hidden behind the doors.
    This version of the game lets you simulate the Monty Hall problem so you can understand the probability behind the game.
    Here you enter the amount of games you'd like to play and how many times you'd like to switch.

Enter Values

    To first start the game, you will have to the amount of times to play the game and the amount of times to switch doors!

    By deafault if nothing is entered, the value will be 0.
    Note: the amount of times to switch doors cannot be greater then the amount of games played.

Run Simulation

    After you have entered in your desired values click "Run Simulation", to simualte the game.

    Note: You cannot simulate the game again, until the previous simulation is over.

Reset Game

    If you would like to reset your stats, click "Reset Game" right next to "Show Stats"

    This will set you back to how the game appeared when you first played.
